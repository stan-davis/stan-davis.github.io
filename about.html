About Me

I'm a game developer with 6+ years of experience, having worked on multiple projects including Simulation Games, First-Person Shooters, Role-Playing Games, and Rogue-Lites.

I primarily work with C# and Unity, and have worked on various projects using C++ with SDL2, DirectX, and Unreal.

I currently am based in Bristol, U.K and work for The Polygon Loft.
